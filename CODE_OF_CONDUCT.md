# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming, respectful, and inclusive environment for everyone involved in this project. We expect all contributors to show empathy, act professionally, and support a safe space—especially considering the sensitive nature of this system.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that help create a positive environment include:

- Using inclusive and respectful language
- Being considerate of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Offering constructive feedback
- Accepting responsibility and apologizing when needed
- Prioritizing the dignity and privacy of individuals represented in the system

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment or discrimination in any form
- Dismissive or degrading comments related to personal characteristics
- Inappropriate or insensitive humor
- Publishing private information without explicit permission
- Downplaying or mocking issues related to gender-based violence

## Our Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying standards of acceptable behavior and are expected to take appropriate and fair corrective action in response to any behavior they deem inappropriate or threatening.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces—online and offline—including the codebase, issues, pull requests, discussions, and any community interactions.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive or unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project team via the appropriate internal channels. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and confidentially.
